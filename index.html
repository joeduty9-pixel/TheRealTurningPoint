About This Page

The time is now to have our Real Turning Point. Let us use this opportunity to turn a lens back on ourselves and have an honest conversation about who we want to be and how we want to be remembered. In recognition of the danger of erasing the dialog of today to adapt to future sensibilities, this page strives to preserve the statements of today, in order to forever accurately represent the true thoughts and feelings of our most influential politicians and personalities.
